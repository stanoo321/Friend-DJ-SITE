KIZER

Dylan Gendelman AKA Kizer has been producing music for over 8 years. After graduating from Icon Music Collective, he went to University of Colorado - Denver to study music production. Kizer specializes in Mid-tempo and electro-funk house but continues to push the genre boundaries. His favorite food is tacos and is a hot sauce aficonado.
